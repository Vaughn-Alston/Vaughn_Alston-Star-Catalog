About the Creator

Hi, I'm Vaughn a Computer Science student with a passion for merging technology and environmental awareness. I created this website to spotlight the effects of light pollution in South LA and help people find the rare spots where the stars still shine.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me working out or spending time with my girlfriend, friends, and family. Being outdoors has always been a big part of my life, and it’s what inspired me to use my tech skills to raise awareness about the environment. In a world that’s constantly moving — whether we’re locked into screens or not — animals go extinct, plants disappear, and stars fade behind artificial light. This project is my way of encouraging others to slow down, look up, and care.

I built this site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as part of my journey to becoming a full-stack developer. I hope it not only helps people discover new places but also inspires them to protect the beauty of our night skies .
